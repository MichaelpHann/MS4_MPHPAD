Find out a little more about what I do....

Weddings

If you’ve made it here, chances are you’re getting married. Congratulations, you guys are obviously pretty fond of each other. I’m gonna go out on a limb and say if you’ve found your way to our little digital space, then you’re probably not after a traditional wedding photographer. It doesn’t matter the style of wedding you’re having whether that’s a church wedding, city hall wedding or eloping on the side of a cliff, I shoot life and love in all its forms.

Family Portraits

Family is just the most special thing. In my own life, I want to celebrate that as often as I can. I understand how fast the time passes and just how precious the memories are. Since starting our family, documenting them develop and grow has become increasingly important. I never want to forget how they were! I’d love to be able to do the same for you.

Headshots

A headshot is more valuable today than it ever has been. It provides instant brand recognition across multiple platforms online like LinkedIn or Twitter etc. I’ve photographed many people, from CEOs to podcast hosts, local business people to social media influencers. I’d love to create your next headshot, either on location or in my Belfast Studio.

Creative Commercial

Your first class product or service deserves a first class image. From high-quality packshots to full production images for advertising, social media such as Instagram and websites, I enjoy working with a range of businesses to guide them through the process of achieving those perfect images. Most importantly, images that sell. I’ve built a strong commercial portfolio spanning interiors, food and drinks, jewellery, fashion and cosmetics industries.
